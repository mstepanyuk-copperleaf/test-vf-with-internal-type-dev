
#### Calculation Details

Rate Ready Organization is computed as:

> Rate Ready Organization Value = RRO1*50

Where:
- Choosing “Will make it possible to prepare rate submission…” in response to question gets RRO1 = 1 and choosing “No impact” RRO1= 0

The constants in the formula are:
- The Optimizer Team determined that making it possible to prepare the rate submission or significantly increase the likelihood of approval is valued at 50 points. This is a configurable parameter.
